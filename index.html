About OSTLI

OSTLI is a simple and powerful app that helps you create, organize, and share your lists. Whether you need to make a grocery list, a to-do list, a wish list, or any other kind of list, OSTLI has you covered. You can easily add items, reorder them, mark them as done, and delete them with simple gestures. You can also customize your lists with different colors, icons, and backgrounds. And you can share your lists with anyone via email, text, or social media.

OSTLI is designed to make your life easier and more productive. You can use it for personal, professional, or educational purposes. You can also sync your lists across your devices and access them anytime, anywhere. OSTLI is the ultimate app for making lists.
